Visualizing rain, water, and greening at the Turner site

A final project for GEOG 456 by Joy Mersmann


This map shows NDVI and precipitation levels over the period of time from January 1st 2021 onward in a 40 by 40 kilometer square area around the Turner and Snodgrass archaeological sites, at which I am doing my dissertation work on local agricultural strategies. Though the local residents and their agricultural practices have changed in the 700+ years since the Turner and Snodgrass sites were occupied, trends in rainfall and greening still impact the area's agricultural fields.
